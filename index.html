Model: the source branch is the pickup (series components); every other branch is a parallel path to ground. Impedances are summed in series within a branch, branches combine as parallel admittances, and the output is the divider H(f)=Zshunt/(Zseries+Zshunt), plotted as magnitude in dB. R = real, L = jωL, C = 1/jωC. A lumped approximation for intuition, not SPICE-exact.
